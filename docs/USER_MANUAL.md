# User Manual
## Behavioural Portfolio Optimizer

### Getting Started

#### Step 1: Create Account
1. Go to http://localhost:3000
2. Click "Register"
3. Enter your name, email and password
4. Click "Create Account"

#### Step 2: View Your Dashboard
1. Go to http://localhost:3000/main
2. See your overall bias score
3. View ML prediction
4. Check your points and level

#### Step 3: Analyze Your Biases
1. Click "Bias Analysis" in navigation
2. View your 8 bias scores
3. Read personalized descriptions
4. Follow the recommended nudges

#### Step 4: Optimize Your Portfolio
1. Click "Portfolio" in navigation
2. Enter your stock symbols
3. Select your bias type
4. Choose optimization method
5. Click "Optimize My Portfolio"
6. View the 3 portfolio comparisons
7. Check Monte Carlo projections

#### Step 5: Get Nudges
1. Click "Nudges" in navigation
2. View personalized nudges
3. Check your badges
4. Accept daily challenge
5. View leaderboard

#### Step 6: Use Trade Journal
1. Click "Journal" in navigation
2. View all your trades
3. Filter by action or market condition
4. Check bias tags on each trade

#### Step 7: Run What-If Simulation
1. Click "Simulator" in navigation
2. Enter your stock symbols
3. Select your bias type
4. Click "Run What-If Simulation"
5. Compare biased vs rational portfolio
6. See how much more you could earn!

### Understanding Bias Scores
- 0-25: Minimal bias (Great!)
- 25-50: Low bias (Good)
- 50-75: Medium bias (Needs attention)
- 75-100: High bias (Take action!)

### Understanding Your Level
- 🌱 Seedling (0-100 pts)
- 🌿 Growing (100-300 pts)
- 🌳 Established (300-600 pts)
- 💎 Diamond (600-1000 pts)
- 🏆 Master (1000+ pts)
